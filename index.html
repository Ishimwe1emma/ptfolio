Emmanuel Ishimwe is a Clinical Officer with strong interests in technology, innovation, and digital transformation in healthcare.

Alongside his medical training, he is actively learning software development through the Power Learn Project (PLP) Academy — gaining expertise in programming fundamentals, problem solving, and modern web technologies.
